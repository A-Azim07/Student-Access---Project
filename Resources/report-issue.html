Report An Issue

Reporting issues is crucial for maintaining a safe and supportive learning environment for all students. Your feedback helps us address concerns promptly and implement necessary measures to ensure everyone's well-being.

If you encounter any of the following situations or have any other concerns, please report them:

  • Harassment or bullying
  • Academic misconduct
  • Safety hazards
  • Discrimination or inequality
  • Any behavior that violates university policies

Your confidentiality is important to us, and all reports will be handled with sensitivity and discretion.
